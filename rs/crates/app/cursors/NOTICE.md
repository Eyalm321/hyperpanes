# Cursor assets

`grab.cur` and `grabbing.cur` are the open-/closed-hand drag cursors from the
Chromium project (`ui/resources/cursors/hand_grab.cur` and `hand_grabbing.cur`),
used because Windows ships no native grab cursor. They are 32×32 with the
hotspot at (13, 13).
